## What is InterChat?

InterChat is a powerful and customizable Discord bot that lets you to create "hubs" or groups where messages sent in a designated channel are broadcasted to other servers within the hub. Whether you want to connect multiple servers under one community or build a network for people with shared interests, InterChat gives you the tools to make it happen.

## Key Features

- **Cross-Server Messaging:** InterChat broadcasts messages to all connected servers within a hub, creating a seamless conversation experience across Discord communities.
- **Moderation Tools:** Equipped with a comprehensive infraction system, InterChat helps keep conversations respectful and aligns with each hub's specific rules.
- **Customizable Word Filters:** Server moderators can block specific words or patterns (with support for both regular expressions and simple word lists) to ensure safe and relevant conversations.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** InterChat offers a straightforward setup process and can be easily managed through Discord's intuitive UI, making it accessible for users of all technical levels.

### Premium Features (Not yet available)

For users looking to get even more out of InterChat, a **premium version** offers enhanced capabilities:

- **Multiple Block Lists per Hub:** Premium users can maintain up to three separate word filter lists per hub.
- **Extended Infraction Management:** Enhanced insights into user history with additional infraction tracking tools.

### Getting Started

If you're new to InterChat, follow these steps:

1. **Invite InterChat to Your Server:** You’ll need server management permissions to set up InterChat. Invite the bot to your server by clicking [here](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=871627754448732692&permissions=8&scope=bot).
2. **Create or Join a Hub:** Connect your server to an existing hub (`/hub join`) or create a new one (`/hub create`) to start sharing messages across communities.
3. **Customize Settings:** Adjust moderation settings, configure word filters, and manage users to align InterChat with your server’s rules. (`/connection edit` if you joined a hub or `/hub` if you created one)

For a deeper dive into setup and customization, continue through this documentation, and feel free to reach out to our support team or community if you need assistance.
