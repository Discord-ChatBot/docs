## \</> New Commands

- `/hub settings` - Toggle hub settings.
- `/hub logging` - Access logging features.
- `/hub blockwords` - Manage prohibited words and phrases.
- `/hub announce` - Announce updates or news to all hub members.
- `/about` - View information about the bot and also credits.
- `Apps > Moderation Actions` - View and take actions on infractions and other mod tasks directly on messages.
- `/hub infractions` - View infractions for a user.
- `/hub appeal set_cooldown` - Set the cooldown for how often users can appeal blacklists. (eg. user can only appeal once every 7 days)
- `/hub visibility` - Set the visibility of the hub. (public or private)
- `/set reply_mentions` - Toggle whether you want to be pinged when someone replies to your messages.
